2. Methodology

This analysis was conducted using a privacy-centric journal insight extraction pipeline. Approximately 650 journal entries from 2024-2025 were processed through DeepSeek v3.2 via OpenRouter's Zero Data Retention mode, extracting structured data about mood, energy, relationships, health, and themes.

The extraction was followed by four tiers of aggregation: weekly summaries, monthly summaries, quarterly "notepads," and a final two-year synthesis. This hierarchical approach enables both granular and high-level pattern discovery.
